ORGB 1100 - Organizational Behaviour
Winter 2022
Presents the study of factors that either influence or are influenced by people at work. The course will focus on macro factors such as organizational structure, technology and environment; group factors such as group dynamics, leadership, conflict, change and decision making; and micro or individual factors such as personality, attitudes, perception and motivation.
ACIT 1630 - Database Systems
Winter 2022
This course covers relational and NoSQL database technologies, including basic concepts, relational algebra, enhanced entity-relationship and NoSQL data modeling, functional dependencies and normalization, design methodology, SQL query language (DDL and DML), views, security and transaction management.
